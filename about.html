Who We Are

KIRA Clinic is a modern private health center located in Nyagatare District. Our mission is to provide patient-centered care that improves the lives of our community through accessible, professional medical services. We offer outpatient treatment, laboratory testing, vaccinations, and health education — all delivered with compassion and professionalism.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, affordable, and quality health services using modern practices, with dignity and respect for every individual.

Our Vision

To be a model community clinic in Rwanda, offering innovative care and serving as a trusted health partner in Nyagatare and beyond.
